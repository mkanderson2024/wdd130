History

White Water Rafting began as a family of white water rafters filled with a passion for rafting. Over time White Water Rafting formed as the family decided to open thier own rafting business.

Dedicated to safe, family friendly adventures, White Water Rafting provides experienced guides each with hours of first hand experience and training.
