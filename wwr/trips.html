Popular Rafting Trips

DetailsA 3 day camping trip, the Grand Staircase Adventure is a fully loaded adventure trip. Rafters start high in the mountains and traverse down 7 steps where the rapids run high and adrenaline even higher. Each step is a unique series of rapids leading up to a short waterfall that drops into a calm pool. Rafters will camp at 3 of these pools. Food will be provided at each camping site along with fun and games.
DetailsA fun and popular day trip, The Bump consits of a quick paced run through two clusters of rapids. Between both clusters is a leasurly area where rafters can swim and enjoy the river. After the second cluster of rapids, the run ends at The Bump, a huge rolling wave that rafters ride over. Pictures are taken at the top of The Bump and are included in the trip.
DetailsA popular 5 day family trip, rafters travel down the Boiling Pot River, known for its roiling rapids that make the water look like its boiling. Hot springs are abundant along the river side, making the water warm and bright blue. Rafters will camp at 4 spots near the largest of the hot springs. Food is provided at each camping site with opportunities to hike and explore the natural lava formations with a guide, soak in the hot springs, and relax after a rigerous run through the rapids.
Details A fun and repeatable day trip, Folding Raft Run is a series of rolling rapids that bend the rafts. Safe for even the newest rafters, rafters lost overboard are able to enjoy coasting through the rapids until the end where they're picked up and brought back to the start. Multiple runs are possible until the end of the day. Lunch is provided.
Details The most challenging trip offered, Bull Run Adventure is a fast paced heart pumping 2 day camping trip filled with alternating heavy rapids and calm rivers. Rafters will work together to get to midpoint lake where they will camp out and enjoy a beautiful lake filled with all sorts of wildlife. Food and hiking guides are provided at the camp.
